An on-chain gaming initiative where players mint NFT Invaders on the Blast network to play a three-phase game, drilling for ETH and LOOKS resources, healing injuries with purchasable Medi-droids, and racing to a planet's core. Features a 500 ETH prize pool, 0.01 ETH mint price, and a maximum 50,000 supply.
