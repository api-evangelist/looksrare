Season 3 concludes with an airdrop distribution of 1,000,000 LOOKS, allocated by the formula G1/G2 × 1,000,000 where G1 is individual Gem count and G2 is total Gem supply. Claims must occur before Season 4 ends or rewards are forfeited to the treasury.
